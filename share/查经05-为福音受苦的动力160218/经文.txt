提摩太后书2章8至13节，和合本、新译本、NIV英文版。
8[和]你要记念耶稣基督乃是大卫的后裔。他从死里复活，正合乎我所传的福音。
[ESV]Remember Jesus Christ, risen from the dead, the offspring of David, as preached in my gospel,
[NIV]Remember Jesus Christ, raised from the dead, descended from David. This is my gospel,
[新]你要记得那从死人中复活的耶稣基督，他是大卫的后裔所生的，这就是我所传的福音。
9[和]我为这福音受苦难，甚至被捆绑，像犯人一样；然而，　神的道却不被捆绑。
[ESV]for which I am suffering, bound with chains as a criminal. But the word of God is not bound!
[NIV]for which I am suffering even to the point of being chained like a criminal. But God's word is not chained.
[新]我为了这福音受了磨难，甚至像犯人一样被捆绑起来；可是，　神的话却不被捆绑。
10[和]所以我为选民凡事忍耐，叫他们也可以得着那在基督耶稣里的救恩和永远的荣耀。
[ESV]Therefore I endure everything for the sake of the elect, that they also may obtain the salvation that is in Christ Jesus with eternal glory.
[NIV]Therefore I endure everything for the sake of the elect, that they too may obtain the salvation that is in Christ Jesus, with eternal glory.
[新]因此，我为了选民忍受一切，好叫他们也可以得着在基督耶稣里的救恩，和永远的荣耀。
11[和]有可信的话说：“我们若与基督同死，也必与他同活；
[ESV]The saying is trustworthy, for: If we have died with him, we will also live with him;
[NIV]Here is a trustworthy saying: If we died with him, we will also live with him;
[新]“我们若与基督同死，就必与他同活；
12[和]我们若能忍耐，也必和他一同作王；我们若不认他，他也必不认我们。
[ESV]if we endure, we will also reign with him; if we deny him, he also will deny us;
[NIV]if we endure, we will also reign with him. If we disown him, he will also disown us;
[新]我们若能坚忍，就必与他一同作王；我们若不认他，他必不认我们；
13[和]我们纵然失信，他仍是可信的，因为他不能背乎自己。”
[ESV]if we are faithless, he remains faithful-- for he cannot deny himself.
[NIV]if we are faithless, he will remain faithful, for he cannot disown himself.
[新]我们纵然不信，他仍然是信实的，因为他不能否定自己。”这话是可信的。
